Reminders sent from: lighthouse@hamilton.edu

To recieve reminder emails, forward emails from lighthouse@hamilton.edu to your desired address.


Forwarding Instructions

  1. In gmail, login to lighthouse@hamilton.edu
  2. On the top right corner of the page select settings, then click See all settings
  3. Click the Forwarding and POP/IMAP or Forwarding tab
  4. In this section click "add a forwarding address", enter the address you want to forward emails to
  5. Click Next, then Proceed, then Okay
  6. In the email account you want to send emails to, click the verification link
  7. Return to the "Settings" page in the lighthouse@hamilton.edu account
  8. Refresh your browser and again click the Forwarding and POP/IMAP or Forwarding tab
  9. Select the radio button that says "forward a copy of incoming mail to" followed by the email you wish to forward to
  10. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Changes
